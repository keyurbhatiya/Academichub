Community Guidelines

Welcome to AcademicHub! Our community thrives on collaboration and respect. Follow these guidelines to ensure a positive and productive experience for everyone.

Be Respectful

Treat all members with kindness and respect. Discriminatory, offensive, or harassing content, including hate speech, bullying, or personal attacks, is strictly prohibited.

No Plagiarism

Share only original work or properly credited content. Plagiarism or unauthorized use of othersâ€™ work, including papers, projects, or blogs, is not allowed.

Keep It Academic

Post content relevant to education, research, or learning. Non-academic or promotional content, such as advertisements or spam, is not permitted.

Report Issues

If you encounter inappropriate content or behavior, report it using the Contact Us form, including a link or description of the issue.

Stay Safe

Do not share private or sensitive information publicly, such as personal contact details, passwords, or financial information.

Follow Upload Guidelines

Upload question papers in PDF format (max 10MB) and projects in ZIP format (max 50MB). Ensure files are relevant, accurate, and free of malicious content.

Respect Content Ownership

Only upload content you have the right to share. Respect the intellectual property of others when using downloaded papers or projects.

Engage Constructively

If commenting or providing feedback, keep discussions constructive and relevant. Avoid off-topic or inflammatory remarks.

Use Accurate Metadata

When uploading papers or projects, provide accurate details (e.g., university, course, semester, or language) to help others find relevant content.

Comply with Moderation

All uploads are subject to moderation. Content violating guidelines may be removed, and repeated violations may lead to account suspension.

Keep Profiles Appropriate

Use appropriate usernames and profile information. Avoid offensive or misleading details in your public profile.

Respect Download Limits

Adhere to any download restrictions or quotas. Excessive downloading or automated scraping is prohibited.

Provide Constructive Feedback

When using the Feedback form, provide clear, constructive, and respectful suggestions to help improve the platform.

No Unauthorized Access

Do not attempt to access accounts, data, or features you are not authorized to use. Hacking or exploiting the platform is strictly prohibited.

Respect Privacy

Do not share or misuse personal information of other users obtained through AcademicHub, such as emails or usernames.

Questions or concerns about our guidelines? Contact Us for assistance.
