Cookie Policy

This Cookie Policy explains how AcademicHub uses cookies and similar technologies to enhance your experience on our platform.

What Are Cookies

Cookies are small text files stored on your device to remember your preferences and improve site functionality.

Types of Cookies We Use

We use essential cookies for login sessions, analytics cookies to track usage, and preference cookies to personalize your experience.

How We Use Cookies

Cookies help us maintain your session, analyze site performance, and customize content based on your interactions.

Managing Cookies

You can disable cookies in your browser settings, but this may affect your ability to use some AcademicHub features.

Third-Party Cookies

We may use third-party analytics tools that set cookies to collect anonymized data about site usage.

Updates to This Policy

We may update this Cookie Policy periodically. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.
