Welcome to the AcademicHub Help Center! Here you’ll find answers to commonly asked questions and resources to help you navigate the platform smoothly. Still stuck? Reach out through our contact form below.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I upload a question paper?

Go to the Papers section, click on "Add New Paper", and fill out the upload form with your file.

How do I create a blog post?

Visit the Blogs section and click “Create Blog”. Use the rich text editor to write and submit your blog for review.

Can I change my profile details?

Yes, go to your profile and click “Edit Profile” to update your name, bio, and other details.

Who can see my uploads?

All uploaded materials are publicly viewable, unless marked private by the admin.
