Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about using AcademicHub, from uploading papers and projects to managing your account and more.

How do I upload a question paper?

Navigate to the “Papers” section, click “Upload Paper,” fill out the form with details like university, course, and semester, and upload your PDF file.

What file formats are supported for paper uploads?

Currently, only PDF files are supported for question paper uploads to ensure compatibility and security.

Can I edit or delete a paper I uploaded?

Yes, go to your profile, select “My Papers,” and use the edit or delete options for your uploaded papers.

How do I search for specific question papers?

Use the filter options in the “Papers” section to select a university, course, or semester to narrow down your search.

Who can upload question papers?

Any registered user, including students, teachers, or researchers, can upload question papers.

How do I upload an academic project?

Go to the “Projects” section, click “Upload Project,” fill out the form with project details, and upload a ZIP file and optional image.

What types of projects can I share?

You can share academic projects in any programming language or discipline, such as coding projects, research papers, or presentations.

Can I download projects shared by others?

Yes, registered users can download project ZIP files from the “Projects” section by clicking the “Download” button.

How do I filter projects by programming language?

In the “Projects” section, use the language dropdown in the filter form to select a specific programming language.

Can I edit or delete a project I uploaded?

Yes, visit your profile, go to “My Projects,” and use the edit or delete options for your uploaded projects.

Who can register on AcademicHub?

Anyone interested in academics, including students, teachers, researchers, or enthusiasts, can register.

How do I create an account?

Click the “Sign Up” button on the homepage, fill out the registration form, and verify your email to activate your account.

Is registration free?

Yes, registering on AcademicHub is completely free for all users.

How do I reset my password?

Click “Forgot Password” on the login page, enter your email, and follow the instructions in the reset email.

Can I update my profile information?

Yes, go to your profile settings to update your name, email, or other personal information.

How do I report inappropriate content?

Use the Contact Us form, provide the link to the content, and describe the issue.

Can I share blog posts on AcademicHub?

Yes, registered users can create and share blog posts via the “Blogs” section in their profile.

Can I edit or delete my blog post?

Yes, visit your profile, go to “My Blogs,” and use the edit or delete options provided.

How do I provide feedback about the platform?

Go to the Feedback page, fill out the form with your name, email, and message, and submit it.

Is my feedback anonymous?

Feedback is not anonymous, as you need to provide your name and email, but your information is kept confidential.

How do I contact support?

Use the Contact Us form to reach our support team with any issues or questions.

Are there file size limits for uploads?

Yes, paper uploads are limited to 10MB, and project ZIP files are limited to 50MB to ensure efficient processing.

Can I download papers without registering?

No, you must be a registered user to download papers and projects for security and tracking purposes.

How are uploaded files moderated?

All uploads are reviewed by our moderation team to ensure they meet AcademicHub’s content guidelines.

Can I share my AcademicHub profile publicly?

Yes, you can share your public profile URL, which displays your uploaded papers, projects, and blogs.

How do I search for content by keyword?

Use the search bar in the “Projects” section to search by title, description, or uploader’s username.

Can I collaborate with others on AcademicHub?

Currently, AcademicHub does not support direct collaboration, but you can share project details and connect via external platforms.

How do I delete my account?

Contact support via the Contact Us form to request account deletion.

Are there premium features on AcademicHub?

Currently, all features are free, but premium features may be introduced in the future. Check our updates for details.

How can I stay updated on new features?

Follow our blog or subscribe to our newsletter via your profile settings to stay informed about new features and updates.

Didn’t find your answer? Contact Us for further assistance.
