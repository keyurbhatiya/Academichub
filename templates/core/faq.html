Frequently Asked Questions

How do I upload a paper?

Go to the “Papers” section and click “Add New Paper.” Fill out the form and upload your file.

Can I edit or delete my blog post?

Yes, visit your profile, go to “My Blogs,” and use the edit or delete options provided.

Who can register?

Anyone interested in academics—students, teachers, or researchers—can register.

How do I report inappropriate content?

Use the Contact Us form and provide the link to the content.
